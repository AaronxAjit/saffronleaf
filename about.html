About

Saffron Leaf brings a splash of vibrancy to the restaurant scene specializing in authentic vegetarian and vegan Indian food. The chefs at Saffron Leaf pride themselves in carefully selecting fresh, local ingredients to create wonderful dishes steeped in aroma and color. Creamy lentil dal, fragrant biryanis-these vegan and vegetarian dishes are designed to bring forth and emphasize the beauty of Indian spices and plant-based ingredients. In developing a menu that focuses on health-conscious cooking with the widest possible range of dietary preferences, Saffron Leaf aims to put care and balance back in the mouth of taste by its famed sticking and original flavors.


Saffron Leaf Co.

Welcome to Saffron Leaf, where we bring the flavors of India to your plate with a vegan/vegetarian twist. Our mission is to provide you with delicious, nutritious, and authentic Indian cuisine that is both healthy and sustainable.
